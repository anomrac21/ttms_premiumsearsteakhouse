All steaks come with a complimentary side salad and any one of the following sides: baked potato, broccoli, fries, corn on the cob, garlic mashed potato, garlic bread, house rice, or parmesan broccoli.

Choose one of the following sauces: red wine reduction, cowboy butter, chimichurri, roasted peppercorn, mushroom sauce, or Secret SEAR.

Cuts 34 oz and above (where applicable) include 2 sides and 2 sauces.

Dry-aged selections are 28–35 days dry aged.

Subject to service charge.
